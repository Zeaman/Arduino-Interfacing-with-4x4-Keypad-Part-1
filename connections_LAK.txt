** Arduino interfaced with 4x4 Keypad, 16x2 LCD & Arduino Mega **

#Connections of the Arduino Mega to LCD:
Arduino Mega			LCD
pin-A0				RS
pin-A1				E
pin-A2				D4
pin-A3				D5
pin-A4				D6
pin-A5				D7
5V				A/no for simulation
				VDD
				Pot
GND				K/no for simulation
				VSS
				RW
				Pot &
				D0-D3
#And other connection:
Pot-middle pin to Vo/VEE of the LCD

pin-4				Row-3
pin-5				Row-4
pin-6				Col-1
pin-7				Col-2
pin-8				Col-3
pin-9				Col-4
